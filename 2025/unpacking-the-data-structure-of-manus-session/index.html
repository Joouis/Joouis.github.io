Unpacking the Data Structure of Manus Session

发表于 2025-03-21 更新于 2025-03-25 分类于 ML/DL/LLM ， AI Agent 阅读时长 ≈ 24 分钟
Simple note of data interface of Manus session with examples.

The data comes from these cases:

  • Comprehensive Tesla Stock Analysis and Investment Insights
  • Comparative Analysis of Travel Insurance Policies
  • Interview Schedule Creation for April 13-15
  • SEO Audit and Optimization Report for Karpathy’s Website
  • Customer Form for B2B Gen AI Consulting Firms
